ServiceNow VTB Enhancer

Configure work item age badges, total work in progress tracking, last updated freshness indicators, and service level expectation targets per board.

Boards inherit defaults unless overridden.

Work Item Age Badges

Adds a color-coded badge to every card showing how many days it has been in progress. Uses Actual start date if available, falling back to Start date, then Opened date.

Each band covers ages up to its Max Days value. The last band (∞) catches everything older. Completed cards always show a green "Done" badge regardless of age.

Total Work in Progress

Sums the card counts across your designated WIP lanes and displays the grand total in the summary bar near the board title.

Total WIP is configured per board — select a specific board from the Board dropdown at the top of this page.

Select the lanes that represent active work in progress on this board.

Last Updated Freshness Indicators

Adds a fresh or stale emoji next to each card's last-updated timestamp, making it easy to spot cards that haven't been touched recently.

Cards updated within this many days show the "fresh" emoji; older cards show the "stale" emoji.

Leave blank to use the defaults (✅ / ❌).

Restrict freshness indicators to specific lanes only

When off, every card on this board shows a freshness indicator regardless of lane. Turn on to limit indicators to specific lanes — useful when you only care about freshness in active work lanes (e.g. "In Flight") and want to ignore backlog or done lanes.

Service Level Expectation (SLE) Target

Sets a maximum age target in days, per board. Cards approaching or breaching the target are highlighted on their age badge and counted in the summary bar near the board title.

SLE is configured per board — select a specific board from the Board dropdown at the top of this page.

Cards older than this many days are considered over the SLE target.

Show an approaching warning when a card is within this many days of the SLE target.

Show summary bar near board title

Displays over/approaching counts inline next to the board name.

Show SLE status emojis on age badge

Prepends an emoji to the age badge as cards approach or breach the SLE target.

Leave blank to use defaults (⚠️ / 🔴).

Show colored border on age badge near SLE

Adds a dashed orange border as cards approach the target and a solid red border when breached.
