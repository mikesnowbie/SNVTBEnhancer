ServiceNow VTB Enhancer

Configure work item age badges, freshness indicators, and service level expectations per board.

Boards inherit defaults unless overridden.

Work Item Age Badge

Shows a color-coded age badge on every card based on the earliest of Actual start date, Start date, or Opened.

Each band covers ages up to its Max Days value. The last band (∞) catches everything older. Completed cards always show a green "Done" badge regardless of age.

Last Updated Freshness Indicator

Adds a fresh or stale emoji next to the "last updated" timestamp at the bottom right of each card.

Cards updated within this many days show the "fresh" emoji; older cards show the "stale" emoji.

Leave blank to use the defaults (✅ / ❌).

Check the lanes where the freshness indicator should appear. Leave all unchecked to show it on every card regardless of lane.

Service Level Expectations (SLE)

Set a day target per board. Age badges show an emoji and border when cards approach or breach the target.

SLE is configured per board — select a specific board from the dropdown above.

Cards older than this many days are considered over SLE.

Show an approaching warning when a card is within this many days of the SLE target.

These emojis are prepended to the age badge when escalation is enabled. Leave blank to use defaults (⚠️ / 🔴).

Show summary bar near board title

Escalate age badge appearance near SLE

Adds an emoji prefix and colored border to the age badge as cards approach (dashed orange) or breach (solid red) the target.
